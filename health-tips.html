Health Tips

🫀 5 Tips for a Healthy Heart

  • Eat more fruits and vegetables
  • Exercise 30 minutes a day
  • Reduce salt and sugar intake
  • Manage stress effectively
  • Stop smoking

🛡️ Boosting Your Immunity

Sleep well, eat balanced meals, stay hydrated, and maintain hygiene to strengthen your immune system.

🍽️ Healthy Diet for Diabetics

Avoid sugary drinks, refined carbs, and instead eat high-fiber, low-GI foods like oats and leafy greens.
